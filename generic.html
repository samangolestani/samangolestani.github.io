More About me

I was born in 1995 in Kerman, a historical city located in South-Eastern part of Iran. In 2006 I was accepted into the NODET (National Organization for Developing Exceptional Talents) school. I spent seven years there until I was admitted to the Amirkabir University of Technology in Tehran at 2013. Living away from family in a dorm room in the 15 Million populated city of Tehran was a great experience, teaching me so many things about life.

I spent the first three semesters of my B.S., learning programming languages and software engineering concepts. I started with C and C++ and then I learned Java programming and developed some applications in an experienced team. Later, I learned Python and some related packages.

Since I used to read Philosophy and Psychology books/novels, I found Cognitive Science very exciting, so I tried to get into the field by watching videos lectures and reading more. Recently, I'm expanding my knowledge in Computational Neuroscience by reading books and papers and participating in Prof. Abdolhossein Abbasian's weekly long discussions in School of Cognitive Science(SCS) of The Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences(IPM).

I also Play Paino since I was 14 years old and it's becoming to my essential hobby to compose and write songs. To check some of my works you can visit my instagram page.
